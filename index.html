About Me

I am from Indore, India. My long-term goal is to use data science to help the Government of India identify citizens in dire need of help and efficiently deliver essential services.

I am currently pursuing my Master's of Science degree in Data Science (MSDS) at the University of San Francisco, where I am in the top 10% of my class with a GPA of 3.82.

I graduated from BITS Pilani in 2015 and worked as a Data Scientist / Assistant Manager at Loylty Rewardz for 5 years. Here, I deployed machine learning algorithms to help our banking clients understand the behavior of their card holding customers and implemented strategies to increase customer stickiness.
